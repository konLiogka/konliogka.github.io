About Me

Hello! My name is Liogka Konstantina , and I am currently pursuing a degree in Information and Electronic Engineering at the International Hellenic University. Residing in the city of Thessaloniki, Macedonia, Greece. I am deeply immersed in the world of technology and engineering.

My academic journey has fueled a diverse range of interests, including Linux operating systems, programming, both backend and frontend development, networking, electronic engineering, and AI/ML. I am constantly trying to better my knowledge on various fields as a way to stay up-to-date with upcoming technologies.

Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, I have a variety of hobbies that enrich my life and personal projects. I enjoy playing the electric guitar, where I find creative expression through music and ideas to implement in my coding/electronic projects. Additionally, photography is a cherished pastime of mine, allowing me to capture the beauty of the world around me and the moments that inspire me. I also greatly enjoy venturing into books/manga, movies and music.
